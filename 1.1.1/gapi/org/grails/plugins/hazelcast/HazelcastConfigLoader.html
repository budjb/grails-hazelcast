Package: org.grails.plugins.hazelcast

[Groovy] Class HazelcastConfigLoader

    • org.grails.plugins.hazelcast.HazelcastConfigLoader

    • Loads Hazelcast configuration from the Grails config and stores them until the service is ready to create the actual instances. Other beans may access the loader before the services does to create instances themselves, depending on bean load order.
